What am I searching?

What am I searching?

Use the drop-down menu to choose how you want to search. Here's what each option means:

Everything: Searches across books, journal articles, media, and more.

Library Catalog (MUSCAT): Finds books, e-books, and media available in Musselman Library.

Course Materials and Reserves: Shows items on reserve for your classes or in the Course Materials collection. You can search by instructor, course number, or title.

Online Resources: Focuses on online content like e-books, journal articles, and streaming media.

Special Collections: Searches unique materials like finding aids, manuscripts, and rare books in Special Collections and College Archives.

Search Other Libraries: Lets you see what other libraries have. You can request books and articles through Interlibrary Loan.
